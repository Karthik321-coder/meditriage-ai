How it works
Three systems.
One platform.
01

Patient Intake & Triage Scoring

Staff enter vitals and symptoms. Our ML model scores each case against ESI and Manchester Triage System protocols, assigning a priority level in under 18 seconds.

02

Live Resource Dashboard

Real-time visibility into bed availability across every department — ICU, trauma, acute care, fast track. Updated every 30 seconds. Accessible from any browser.

03

Predictive Shortage Engine

Forecasts resource shortfalls 2–4 hours ahead using admission patterns and current intake rate. Triggers early alerts before a crisis hits, giving hospitals time to respond.

Features
Everything an ER needs.

ESI + Manchester Triage

Dual-protocol scoring using the two most clinically validated triage systems globally. Not a custom model — built on proven medicine.

Real-time bed tracking

Live occupancy across all departments. Colour-coded utilization bars make critical bottlenecks visible at a glance.

Smart alert system

Tiered alerts for critical ESI-1/2 patients, department overloads, and forecast risk events. No alert fatigue — only what matters.

6-hour forecasting

Predictive resource utilization model that forecasts surges before they happen. Colour-coded risk levels from low to critical.

Vital signs anomaly detection

Automatic flagging of critical vitals against clinical reference ranges. Compound risk scoring for multi-parameter abnormalities.

Zero-infrastructure deployment

Runs in any modern browser. No hospital IT required. Designed for district hospitals and PHCs with limited infrastructure.
